Discover All Mask Styles

Full grain leather masks with detachable 100% cotton face coverings

Mask features bendable nose strip, 2 ply cotton fabric with pocket insert for optional filter(not included). Cloth portion of mask is washable, leather ear straps feature ring snaps and are detachable.

We make these masks to order by hand in our small studio in Brooklyn. Please allow 2 days for production and processing.

We ship 100% plastic free!

Ships Worldwide

We ship our masks domestically and worldwide using insured shipping carriers including USPS, UPS, and DHL to ensure your product arrives in a safe and timely fashion.

One of a Kind

Our most common feedback is how unique our product is. We combine form and function for a face mask that is tailored and classic but also uses quality durable materials for added protection and to withstand heavy use.

Customizable

Featuring a modular design, clients can pick and choose their components from a wide swathe of fine italian leathers, hardware, and fabric color/type. Custom logo embossing is available for a one time set-up fee.

Handmade in NYC

All of our manufacturing is done at our studio located in the creative pulse of New York: Brooklyn, NY. By placing an order with us, you are supporting an LGBT, woman and owned small business.
